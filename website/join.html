Community Directives

  1. Respect the Network: Toxicity, harassment, or discrimination will result in an immediate global ban.
  2. Clear Communications: Ensure voice channels and general chats remain unobstructed during active operations or weather alerts.
  3. No False EAS Intel: Generating fake emergency alerts or falsely portraying Weather.gov data is a zero-tolerance offense.
  4. Follow the Chain of Command: During official Staff-led ROBLOX SCR shifts or stream events, follow instructions from active Dispatchers.
  5. Have Fun: We are a sanctuary for weather nerds and rail enthusiasts. Connect, share, and enjoy!
